Energy cells explained

The “Verband der Elektrotechnik, Elektronik und Informationstechnik” (VDE) proposes a new energy system model framework based on decentralized energy system. This framework named the cellular approach is the idea that the production and consumption of energy are balanced in a so-called energy cell. The basic concept of an energy cell incorporates the notion that there is no geographical delimitation to it and therefore multiple energy cells interacting with each other can be considered as one bigger cell (different zoom levels). An energy cell can contain many form of energy such as electricity, heat, gas and mobility thus embracing the concept of sector coupling.
